Our Story

A Passion for
Fire & Flavor

Born from a belief that great cooking begins with great fire, Ember & Ash was founded by Chef Marcus Veillard in 2019. Every dish is kissed by our custom-built wood-burning hearth — sourcing only the finest seasonal produce from local Hudson Valley farms.

Our philosophy is simple: let the ingredients speak, let the fire transform, let every meal become memory.
